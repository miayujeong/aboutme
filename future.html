What does data analyst do?

Data analysts gather, cleanse, analyze data, and uncover business insights. They reviews data to identify key insights and ways the data can be used to solve problems. Also, they communicate this information to company leaders and other stakeholders.

Data analysts need skills such as:

technical skills

  • statistical programming
  • machine learning
  • data visualization

soft skills

  • domain knowledge
  • communication
  • presentation
